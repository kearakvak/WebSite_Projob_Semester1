SUMMARY OF ERN MEMBERS INTERESTS



ERN members' interests include disability policy; health systems; P&O and bio-engineering; P&O device use, outcomes and effectiveness; disability measurement and statistics; methodologies for including people with disabilities; health economics; mental health and trauma; quantitative and qualitative research and methodologies; market research; gender equality; poverty and social inequality and rehabilitation.
